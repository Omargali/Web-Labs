Sandyk is the original, Kazakh name of a unique household item, one of the oldest types of furniture, in which precious items for each family were stored and transported. The purpose of our restaurant with the same name "Sandyk" is to show the centuries-old traditions both in cooking, serving dishes, hospitality in general, and to present unique objects of historical and cultural significance, household items of the South Kazakhstan region.

INTERIOR

Unique
interior

The southern region of Kazakhstan has been famous since ancient times for wood carving, pottery and carpet weaving, zergers and weaving, mat weaving and felting, sewing and embroidery. We draw the attention of our visitors to the types of crafts that were once widely known outside the country - glassblowing and leatherworking, individual items and details of which, today, adorn the halls of the restaurant.
